New makeover for 2022

Minimalist, scraped out a few things and added some new stuff.

I have been reading a lot than writing these days, thanks to my hectic schedule, I barely get the time to even look at my blog.

Previously, I used to have different sections of articles such as Writings and Today I Learned but now, it hit to me, why did I even create such sections if both of them are supposed to represent my learning experiences?

So, from here onwards, I decided to merge both sections into one and I call it as a "Weblog". And also, thanks to Simon Willison's Blog, I took the idea of "Blogmarks" and it's basically a collection of articles and quotes that I found interesting and would share it here.

After taking some feedback from some of my colleagues and friends, I added features like search and tags to help users find content easier.

I did a few tweaks to my blog engine and the UI to make it look more minimal and to the point, while I still retained the colors and fonts used in the previous layout (Thank you, Rasmus for the amazing Inter font ‚ù§).

I hope you like the new look of this site.
